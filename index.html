What is Founders Dry Clean?

Simple and straight: it is a service where you drop off you suits, shirts, pants, blouses,... and we take care of the rest! You can then come back and pick up your items Sunday night at the BIF.

Our Guarantee

A large concern when met when discussing this venture was lost or damaged items. We have taken numerous steps to mitigate this risk including: purchasing professional tagging equipment, refining our processing flow, and never storing your items in personal locations.

We wll also make good on any lost or damaged items, giving you reparations up front, placing the burden on ourselves to pursue claims with the responsible party.

Our Pricing

No markup.

That's right. We don't charge a delivery fee or markup for our service. Our margin comes from the volume discount we are receiving from our cleaning partner.

What doesn't go into operating costs will all be pumped back into Founders!
